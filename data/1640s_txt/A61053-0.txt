

TO THE MOST HIGH AND MOST POTENT MONARCH, IAMES, OF GREAT BRITAIN, FRANCE, AND IRELAND KING; THE MOST CONSTANT AND MOST LEARNED DEFENDER OF THE FAITH; INLARGER AND UNITER OF THE BRITISH EMPIRE; RESTORER OF THE BRITISH NAME; ESTABLISHER OF PERPETUALL PEACE, IN CHURCH AND COMMON-WEALTH; PRESIDENT OF ALL PRINCELY VERTUES AND NOBLE ARTS:
JOHN SPEED, HIS MAJESTIES MOST lowly and most loyall Subiect and Servant, consecrateth these his Labours, though unworthy the aspect of so high an Imperiall Majestie.
