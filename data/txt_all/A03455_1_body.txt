


TO THE RIGHT HIGH & MIGHTIE CHARLES, OF GREAT BRITAINE, France, and Jreland; the first King (of that Name) and second Monarch: Defender of the FAITH, &c.
Sole Inheritor of his Royall Fathers KINGDOMES and VERTVES. AND To King IAMES his Jmmortall Memorie.
This Elegie is Consecrated by his Sacred Majesties humblest and meanest Subject.
