



HAVING (as I humbly conceive) found out a sure Method for the well Manning Your Majesties Fleet, (the Srength and Glory of Your Dominions, and Terror of Your Enemies) on any Occasion in a Months Time, without the Trouble or Charge of Impressing; As also a Method for the Breeding of Seamen, and Encouraging Navigation, whithout Charge to Your Majesties; I humbly presume to lay the same at Your Majesties Royal Feet, in hopes of Your Gracious Acceptance and Countenance of my Endeavours, for the Service of Your Majesties, the Ease of Your Subjects, and the General Benefit of Trade.
