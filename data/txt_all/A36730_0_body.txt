


TO THE Supream, Most High and Mighty Prince HARLES II.
By the Grace of God, OF GREAT BRITAIN, FRANCE, and IRELAND KING, DEFENDER of the FAITH, &c.
THIS ATLAS CHINENSIS, CONTAINING Remarkable Passages IN TWO EMBASSIES FROM NEW BATAVIA TO KONCHI, EMPEROR OF China and East-Tartary.
WITH A MORE EXACT GEOGRAPHICAL DESCRIPTION THAN FORMERLY, DEDICATED with all HUMILITY, LIES PROSTRATE AT THE SACRED FEET OF YOUR MOST SERENE MAJESTY, BY THE HUMBLEST OF YOUR SERVANTS, AND MOST LOYAL SUBJECT, IOHN OGILBY.
