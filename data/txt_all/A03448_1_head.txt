❧To the Right Honorable, and his singular good Lord and maister, S. William Brooke Knight, Lord warden of the cinque Portes, and Baron of Cobham, all increase of the feare and knowledge of God, firme obedience towarde his Prince, infallible loue to the common wealth, and commen∣dable renowne here in this wolde, and in the worlde to come, lyfe euerlasting.
