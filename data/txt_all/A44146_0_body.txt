



Honoured Sir,
THE satisfaction that was exprest at the hearing of this Sermon, and the Candid entertainment it hath found since, have induc'd me to gratifie the requests of some, in making it more pub∣lick; which I the rather comply'd with, because it gave me an opportunity to publish to your Honour, and the World, my grateful resentment of your great and unmerited favours. I am not so vain as to imagine that any thing therein deliver'd should be thought able to present your Honour with any in∣struction, or make the least addition to the ample stock of your present knowledge; if it may prove only a remem∣brancer, or upon any other account may be esteem'd worthy your Honours perusal or acceptance, the Author hath attain'd his end, and will rest satisfied in the suc∣cess
