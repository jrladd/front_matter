TO THE MOST HIGH AND PVISSANT PRINCE, CHARLES by the Grace of God, the first of that Name, and second Monarch of the whole Iland of Great BRITTAINE: His vndoubted Royalties being vnited vnder one and the same his most glorious Crowne, the Kingdoms of England, Scotland, France, and Ireland; Gods Immediate Vice Gerent; Supreame head of all Persons, and Defender of the true, ancient Christian Faith, in these his Empires and Dominions.
