TO THE MOST HIGH AND PVISSANT Prince, King Iames, by Gods grace King of Englād, Scotland, France, and Ireland defendor of the Faith, &c. To the most renowned and vertuous Ladie our gratious Soveraigne, Queene Anne his wife: both their heartes desires in our Saviour Christ whilst they live and raigne vpon the earth, and after∣wardes a Diademe of perfect glorie.
