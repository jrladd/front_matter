To the Right VVorshipfull, My much Honoured Friends,

Collonel FRANCIS WEST, Lieutenant of the Tower of London.
Mr. WILLIAM CLEGAT, Master of Katharines Hospitall neere the Tower.
Mr. SOLOMON SMITH, Marshall of the High Court of the Admiralty of England.
 Esquires, Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex. Mercy, and Peace, and Love, be multiplyed.
