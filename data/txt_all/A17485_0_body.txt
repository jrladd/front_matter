



SIR, I entreate you, patronize this smal Treatise, if in your iudgement it can either pleasure, or in small measure profite, by being divulged to the o∣pen view of the world: If other∣wise, lay it by you as a bundle of waste paper. In so doing, I shal thinke my selfe much obliged vnto you: if in suppressing the originall, my weakenesse may rather take a priuate checke of a friend for being too bolde, then an open scorne of a multitude, for being too foolish.
