❧ TO THE MOST NOBLE, MOST MYGHTYE, AND MOST redoubted prysnte kynge HENRY the VIII. kynge of Englande and of Fraunce, defensour of the fayth▪ lorde of Ireland, and in erth supreme heed immediatly vn∣der Chryst of the churche of England, his humble seruaunt RYCHARD TAVERNER desireth all Ioye, felycitie, and longe lyfe.
