


FOrasmuch good tossen, as the rare exam∣ple of so terrible a fact, sent vnto you, and written in the french tongue, hath happe∣ned vnto my handes by youre meanes, I coulde not but translate the same into our eng∣lish languedge, aswell to giue you knowledge of that which was sent vnto you, as also to make plaine and open to all men, the moste straunge, rare, and horrible facte, latelye committed by a yong man of the age of two or three and twenty yeares, as hereafter shall appeare, the executi∣on and punishment, of whom, I wish may be an example to all such as are so vnmindfull of God and godlinesse or that are any waye tempted to offend in the like, praying God to blesse & keepe vs in health, and graunt all men to haue this worthy saying in memory, whatsoeuer thou ta∣kest in hand, remember the end, and thou shalt neuer doe amisse, written this eight of March. 1586.
