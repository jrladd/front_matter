

TO THE RIGHT HONOVRABLE, THOMAS, LORD VISCOVNT FENTON, CAPTAINE OF THE ROYALL GVARD; ONE OF HIS MAIESTIES MOST HONOVRABLE PRIVY COVNSELLORS;
