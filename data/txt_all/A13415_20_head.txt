DEDICATED
To theReader, you must note, that this Gentleman did send me from the Fairne Iland, a barrell of Gulls and Cormotant egges, by 
ing of which, I haue attained to the vnderstanding of many words which our Gulls and Cormotants doe speaks here abou, Sensible, Reasonable, Affable, Amiable, Acceptable,
minded, Honourable, in VVit, Iudgement, and Vnderstanding Able, Robert
Rugge Gentleman, Reare Adelantado of the Holy Iland, the Fairne, and
the Staples, on the Coast of Northumbria.
