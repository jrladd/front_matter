To the English Nation, and to every Individual Person
within these Brittish Isles, Geo. Wither, wishes that
Grace, Peace, and Love in CHRIST JESUS,
which by an unfeigned Humiliation, may reconcile us
all to GOD; Vnite us by an indissolvable Band of
Charity, to each other; and in a Christian Amity,
withall Nations through the world.
