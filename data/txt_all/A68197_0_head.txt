To the Right Honorable, and his singular good
Lord and Maister, S. William Brooke Knight, Lord Warden of
the cinque Ports, and Baron of Cobham, all increase of the feare
and knowledge of God, firme obedience toward his Prince,
infallible loue to the common wealth, and commendable
renowme here in this world, and in the world to come
life euerlasting.
