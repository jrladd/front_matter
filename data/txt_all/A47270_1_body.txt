AFter mature deliberation, what to tender unto your ac∣ceptance worthy your Patron∣age, nothing occurred more probable, than this small Manuall; which was once esteemed a rich Cabinet of know∣ledge, by a person truely Honourable. May it auspitiously procure but your Honours like friendly Estimation, and then I doubt not, but it will find a uni∣versal acceptance amongst persons of greatest Eminency. Sure I am, it may be justly deemed as a rich maga∣zne of experience, having long since

taught the world its approved excel∣lency, yea, even in many dangerous exi∣gencies.
