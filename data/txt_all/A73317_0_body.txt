



SIR;
It was a season for Elegies of this kind, when I wrote this; before HIS Funeralls. I had no purpose (then) to haue it published. Importuni∣ty hath (since) drawne it from me. But my first intent of Dedication is not altered. It cannot; vnlesse I could change my selfe. And (besides the subscription of my duety to you) you deserue to bee acknowledged in this Argument; among HIS true Mourners: for you honoured HIM, much and faithful∣ly.
