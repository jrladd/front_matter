


Masters,
YOU are now in your Winter Quarters; I have there∣fore sent you this Pamphlet to warm you. Not that I intend to play at Blow-coals with you, you know that sport too well. Only, finding your Genius given to new Doctrines (many of you being Seekers) I thought a Paradox might not not be unwelcome, one allowes killing, another lying; such Treatises every foot come to your hands; sure then you will accept of this Piece; that smells much of your Match and Gunpowder, and concerns more your Strength, and your Trade.
