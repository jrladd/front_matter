To my worthy, and Right welbeloved Freinds, Maister Caleb Cockcroft, Treasurer to the famous followship of Merchant Adventurers of England, resident at Delft And vnto Maister Iohn Barkelay. Maister Arthur Tyndall. Maister Iohn Quarlys. Maister William Clarke. Bretheren of the same Society Iohn Wing, wisheth all present welfare, and prosperity o earth, which may further their future felicity in heaven.
