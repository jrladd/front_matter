



WIth the same deuotion, and integrity of vnspotted affection (most Noble Lord) I present to your gracious accep∣tance this fourth Booke of Amadis de Gaule, encouraged thereto by that truly vertuous Lady, who hath promi∣sed, to stand betweene me and your dis-fauour, in presu∣ming so boldly as herein I haue done. The fift and sixt Bookes are shortly to succeed, vpon your Honorable ac∣ceptation of these: and whatsoeuer else remaineth in my poore power, either in these or the like imployments, are dedicated soly to your Noble seruice.
