TO THE RIGHT Honourable, ELIANOR RICH, Countesse of Sussex and Warwick, Vicountesse Baronesse Fitzwalter, Lady Egremond, Burmel, Mortimer, and Leez, Beloved Consort to the Right Honorable, Robert, Earle of Warwick, Baron of Leez, and Lord High Admirall of England: JEREMIAH RICH wisheth health here, and happinesse hereafter.
