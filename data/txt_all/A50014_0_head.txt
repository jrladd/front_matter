TO THE Right Honourable, and Truly Noble, HENRY Lord Viscount Sydney of Shepey, Baron Milton, Lord Lieutenant of Kent, Constable of Dover Castle, Lord Warden of the Cinque Ports, Master General of Their Majesties Ordnance, Colonel of Their Majesties First Regiment of Foot Guards, one of the Gentlemen of His Majesties Bedchamber, and one of the Lords of Their Majesties Most Honourable Privy Council, &c.
