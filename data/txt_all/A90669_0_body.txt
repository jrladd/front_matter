


TO THE WORSHIPFULL, Mr. RICHARD LITHGOLD, Mr. JOHN CHILD, Bailiffs of the ancient Corporation of Kingston upon Thames:
For their endeavour for the publique good; For their vigilancy in their Place, and Office:
Especially, For the assistance, and encouragement, of their Minister, in preaching the Word, and suppressing novell Fancies:
THIS IS DEDICATED, Willingly, Deservedly.
