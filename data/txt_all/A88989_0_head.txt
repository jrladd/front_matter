TO THE HIGH AND HONOVRABLE COVRT OF PARLIAMENT: And by Name, (As to them, to whom he is most obliged) TO Sir Herbottle Grimstone, Knight and Baronet. Mr. Maurice Barow, Esquire, his ever honoured patron. And Mr. Herbottle Grimstone, Esquire. JOHN MAYER, doth in all humility, dedicate this his labour in the Lord.
