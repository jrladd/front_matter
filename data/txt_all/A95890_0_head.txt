TO THE
RIGHT HONOURABLE
Sir IOHN WOLLASTONE,
Knight; Lord Major of the renowned
City of LONDON:
AND
To the Right Honourable and truly
elect Lady, the Lady WOLLASTONE, his
most vertuous and truly pious Consort; J. V. most humbly and heartily prayeth all
encrease of Honour here, and the blessed assurance
of Heaven hereafter.
