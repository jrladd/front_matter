


THE DEDICATION, TO THE SINNERS and SAINTS IN ENGLAND, especially in EXON and TIVERTON.
The AUTHOR'S
(As a publick and permanent testimony of their unfeigned desires, that they may be found walking in the narrow way, that leadeth unto life, and that so they may be saved)
Devote the ensuing EXHORTATION & DIRECTIONS,
Earnestly beseeching the God of all grace, that their Patronage may be the living influ∣ences of the quickning Spirit, who only is able to protect them from unprofitable rea∣ding, and forgetful neglects, and to make them effectual to their Desirable, and Desired ends.
