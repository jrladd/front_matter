



To my Dear and Loving Wife.
WIFE, in my Epistles to my two former Books I was very large; the Porch was too big for the House: you shall have that fault mended in this, and I shall only intreat you to accept of the Dedication of it; not knowing any Person whom I love more than your self; nor any to whom I am more indebted. Thus in few words I take my leave, and rest
