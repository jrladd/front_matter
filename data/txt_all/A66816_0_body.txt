


TO THE HIGHLY BORN, HONORABLY MINDED. AND WORTHY OF HONORABLE AND HIGH RESPECT, MATTHEW HALSE of Efford in the County of Devon Esquire, THEOPHILUS WODENOTE one of his obliged Oratours dedicates these his Aphorisms, or Bre∣viats of Speculations; And daily beseecheth God for him, that both he, and all his issue, so specious already by affinitie with so many Noble Proge∣nies, and still growing more and more eminent by their own sin∣cere and abundant piety and charity, may by di∣vine mercy be pre∣served, thorough faith, unto sal∣vation.
