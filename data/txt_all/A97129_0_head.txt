To the Right VVorshipfull Master Thomas
Clarke, and Master Thomas Cart, Bailiffes
of TEUKSBURIE:
And
To Master William Wilson, Master William Croste, Master Thomas
Hale, Mr. Theoph: Ally Mr. Iohn Millington, Mr. Henry Lane,
Mr. Thomas Clarke junior, Mr. Edward Phelps; and to all
the rest of the well affected to King and
Parliament there:
Iohn Ward wisheth true peace and freedome here,
and etel felicity hereafter.
