



DEare SIR Robert Cotton, this little Treatise hath a long time, (according to HORACES aduice) layen in season with my owne iudgement. And now it desires to come forth into the Worlds vse. from your acceptance. Pray Sir re∣ceiue it kindly, as you haue done me for the space of more then foure yeares,
cherishing both my Life and Lear∣ning. So, if Gods pleasure bee to finde mee out a happie leasure, I shall euer striue to expresse greater thank∣fulnesse, and rest,
