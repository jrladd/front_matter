


RIGHT HONOVRABLE,
IT were needlesse to commend the worth of musicke to a noble and vn∣derstanding disposition: for in the natures of Artes and generous spirites, ther is a sympathie, this being only grac'd by them; and they the onely patrones of this profession: I haue presumed, though not worthy your acceptance (as to the fauourer of all vertue) humbly to entreate your Lordship to patronize these my simple labours, which if your Lordship vouchsafe, they haue their hire, and my self euer bound (as is my duty) to doe your Lordship all faithfull, dutifull and acceptable ser∣uice.
