To the moste vertuouse, myghtye, and excellente Prynce, Edwarde the.vi. by the grace of God King of England, Fraunce, & Ireland, defender of the faith, and in earth vndre Christ, of the Churches of the sayd England & Irelande ye supreme head, his moste humble subiecte Iohn Bale wysheth all honour, helthe, and felycyte.
