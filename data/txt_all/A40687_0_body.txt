



IT grieved me when I was to perform the last office to our deceased Friend, that I had scarce the stump of a voice left me, so that very few did distinctly hear what I did deliver. This hath made me the more willingly condescend to your desire in Printing this Sermon, that your Eye may peruse what your Ear did not receive. And as you have honoured your dead Friend in attending his Corpse in so sad and solemn an equipage, so you
