






John Vicars moſt humbly Dedicateth and Conſecrateth
Himſelf and Theſe moſt Gratefull Memorialls
of Gods Eternall Glory; and Their high Honour;
And ever prayes (as he is moſt juſtly bound)
for a moſt plentifull Repay of enlarged Gracious Honour,
heer, and Glorious Immortality, heerafter, (For
all their pious Paines and indefatigable Labours, Loyalty
and Love, for the Beſt-Good of Church and
State) to bee ſhowred downe into the Boſomes of
Them, and their Poſterity, after them, for ever.
