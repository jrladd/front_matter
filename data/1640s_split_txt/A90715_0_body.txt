with Controverſies amongſt the Learned
in Divinity. And therefore it▪ is likely that ſome will
ſay of me, as it was ſomtime ſaid of one, Thou art but
a ſtripling, but they are men of War from their youth▪ and therefore
not to be encountred with by thee. And they will think it
