To the Right Honourable and mighty Lord Edmund by Divine Providence Lord Proprieter, Earl Palatine, Governour and Captain Generall of the Province of New Albion, and to the Right Honourable the Lord Vicount Monſon of Caſtlemain, the Lord Sherard Baron of Letrim: and to all other the Vicounts, Barons, Baronets, Knights, Gentlemen, Merchants, Adventurers, and Planters of the hopefull Company of New Albion, in all 44 undertakers and ſubſcribers, bound by Indenture to bring and ſettle 3000 able trained men in our ſaid ſeverall Plantations in the ſaid Province. Beauchamp Plantagenet of Belvil in New Albion Eſquire, one of the Company, wiſheth all health, happineſſe, and heavenly bleſſings.
