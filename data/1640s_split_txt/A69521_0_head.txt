TO MY MVCH HONOVRED, THE two vertuous young Gentlewomen, Mris
Elionor Carent, and Mris
Elionor Warre, Cousin Germans in the flesh, Concordants in the Spirit, and Co-inhabitants under the domesticke government of the Right Worshipfull, and truly religious Matron Mris
Amy Long widow, of Steeple Ashton, in the County of Wilts, my singular good friends, and neighbours, peace of conscience, and joy in the Holy Ghost, be multiplied here in this life, and endlesse glory be assured hereafter, in the life to come.
