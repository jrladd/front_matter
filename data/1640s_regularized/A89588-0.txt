TO THE RIGHT HONOURABLE John Warner Lord Major of the City of LONDON . My Lord , THis plain Sermon was preached in obedience to the call I received from you , it is publiſhed according to your requeſt , and that it may be ( though in never ſo weak a meaſure ) a strengthening of your hands in your great work , it is humbly preſented unto you as a pledge of the unfeigned reſpect and ſervice of Your Lordship's much obliged STEPHEN MARSHAL .
