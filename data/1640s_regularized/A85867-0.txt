To his ever honoured Valentine VVauton Eſquire , Colonel , and one of the Honourable Houſe of Commons . As alſo to the other worthy Gentlemen ; together with all the good people of the Pariſh of Great Staughton in the County of Huntington . SIR , YOU hereſee , what the the reſt of the Pariſh herd ; under one months occasional pains in my place . I am forced to make it public , partly becauſe I ſuffered ſome oppoſition & affront in my preaching , from one I never ſaw before ; and partly becauſe ſome opinions of it are ſpread ſince , by and among thoſe that know me not . But chiefly to anſwer the expectation of thoſe amongſt whom I am known . I hope ( what I here tender you ) will speak the Truth of and honeſt Conſcience to the World ; the Affection of a careful Paſtor to the reſt ; and to you Sir ! the grateful ( becauſe much engaged ) mind of Your humble Servant , JOHN GAULE .
