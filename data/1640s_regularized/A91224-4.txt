TO THE RIGHT Worshipful his very Noble and highly Honoured good Friend , SIR WILLIAM BALFORE Knight , Lieutenant of the Tower of LONDON . SIR , it were Ingratitude and Injury , For me in ſilence here to pass you by , And not preſent theſe Cordials unto you , Sith they , and all my ſervice are your due , For all your Noble Favours in the Tower , Which have engaged me to my utmoſt power , To be your eternally devoted poor Friend and ſervant , WILLIAM PRYNNE .
