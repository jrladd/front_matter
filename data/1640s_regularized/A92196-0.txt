TO THE Moſt Illuſtrious PRINCE CHARLES , Prince of Great BRITAINE , Duke of Cornwall and Aubigny , Earl of Chester , etc. May it pleaſe Your Highness , This humbly conceived the duty of all the youth of ENGLAND to dedicate themſelves and their endeavours to your HIGHNESSE : not only in regard of your Native but Acquired Greatness , which draws the eyes of all Good Men upon Your HIGHNESSE Perſon and Actions . This Sir makes me humbly beg leave to lay my firſt fruits at Your HIGHNESSE feet , which ( without farther Preſumption ) is the utmoſt Ambition of Your Highness moſt humble and moſt faithfully devoted JOHN RAYMOND .
